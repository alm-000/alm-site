## Overview

I partnered with regional compliance teams to design user journeys that met regulatory expectations without creating excessive friction for users.

## What I Delivered

I redesigned flows around KYC, AML, risk assessment, disclosures and document gathering. I clarified language, simplified decision pathways and ensured each journey aligned with regulators across different markets.

## Impact

The updated flows passed regulatory review with fewer revisions, and users experienced a more streamlined, predictable onboarding process.
