## Overview

I worked on improving Exinity’s payment infrastructure to increase speed, reliability and coverage across global markets.

## What I Delivered

I delivered upgrades to the transaction processing workflow, improved error handling and led the integration of new payment service providers. I also refined the UX around deposits, withdrawals and failed transactions.

## Impact

Transaction speed improved by around 10 percent and customers gained access to a more reliable, region-appropriate set of funding options.
