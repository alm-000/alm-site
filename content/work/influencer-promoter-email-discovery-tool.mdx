## Overview

I built a tool that finds and enriches emails for promoters, creators and potential partners by scraping public websites.

## What I Delivered

The system crawled sites, extracted emails, cleaned and verified leads, enriched metadata and synced everything into a CRM. It also generated categorisation labels to prioritise outreach.

## Impact

This became a reliable pipeline for partner acquisition across Dansu and other projects.
