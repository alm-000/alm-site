## Overview

I created and developed Dansu’s wearable towel concepts: the Bandolier Towel, WristWrap Towel, Pocket Loop Towel and Modular Gym Towel.

## What I Delivered

I designed functional prototypes, tested materials, built use-case frameworks for gym, running and festivals, and shaped a modular product language that differentiated Dansu from standard towel brands.

## Impact

These products introduced a defensible angle to the brand and opened new categories with higher margin and stronger storytelling potential.
