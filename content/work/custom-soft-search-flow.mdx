## Overview

I redesigned the soft-search eligibility flow to give users clearer guidance and reduce confusion about checks, approval signals and credit impact.

## What I Delivered

I rebuilt the journey logic, refined the hierarchy of information, clarified credit-impact messaging and produced cleaner eligibility screens. I also tightened API requirements and error states to reduce drop-offs.

## Impact

Users gained more confidence in the process, approval clarity increased and early-stage abandonments decreased.
