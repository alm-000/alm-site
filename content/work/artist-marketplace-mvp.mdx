## Overview

I built the MVP for Everynite, a marketplace connecting artists with venues and event organisers. The goal was to validate demand quickly with a functional booking platform.

## What I Delivered

I designed and shipped the core platform, including artist profiles, availability calendars, booking requests and messaging. I integrated Stripe Connect to handle payouts, onboarding and revenue splits.

## Impact

The MVP validated the business model, processed real bookings and provided the foundation for the marketplace pivot.
